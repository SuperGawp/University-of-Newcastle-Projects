BALI GETAWAY

Take a week off and go explore the beautiful culture of DPS! The scenery is gorgeous and there is nothing better than just relaxing and lazing around a pool or beach. Try out the food! It's absolutely delicious, nothing beats eating Nasi Goreng for lunch, such a simple dish but will fill you up greatly and of course, taste amazing as well. Later on take a trip to the temples and immerse yourself in the stunning culture.



SAKURA MOMENT

There is nothing as pretty as the scenery in JAP. You and your parner can spend your honeymoon strolling the streets of Kyoto and enjoying the gorgeous architecture and don't forget all the food that can be tried, the choises are endless! At the end spend 2 nights in one of the most beautiful cities in the world, Osaka. Roam around the city, shop till you drop, eat as much food as you possibly can! Or just simply take a walk with your partner and just admire the scenery. What more could you want?
